FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Is this secure?

Yes, we take security VERY seriously so your online to do list is always protected. First, HTTPS is used to for all communication between the browser and our servers. HTTPS stands for "Hypertext Transfer Protocol Secure" (quite a mouthful) means that all the data is encrypted/scrambled so no one can see your information. In addition, your information is password protected so no one else can access your information. We use Stripe for payment processing which is the industry leading solution. With Stripe, none of your credit card information is ever sent to us. Stripe receives your payment information and then provides us with a token showing that you've paid.

When do completed items disappear?

Completed items show in your online to do list for 5 days and then they are hidden.

How are reoccurring to dos handled? What is Frequency?

Reoccurring to dos is a powerful feature in your online to do list. When a reoccurring to do is completed a new to do is automatically created with the correct due date. For example, if you have a to do "pay the cable bill" with a Frequency of Monthly, when you complete the item on October 2nd, a new to do is automatically created for November 2nd.

I have an idea for a new feature. Can I make a request?

Absolutely! We would love to hear your suggestions, in fact, some of our best features were the result of user suggestions. Submit your idea through the "Contact Us" page. Thanks in advance!

Is it possible to add Priority and/or Due Date during the quick add?

Yes. You can include Priority, Category, Due Date and Frequency when adding a to do. Just separate each field with the "|" field For example: "Pay Cable Company|10/15/2014|2|Bills" will add a to do named "Pay Cable Company" with a Due Date of "10/15/2014", a priority of "2" and a category/tag of "Bills"
