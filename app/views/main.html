Welcome to Todo Giant!

How will this help me?

A todo list is one of the simplest steps you can take to becoming more organized and productive. It focuses you on the key tasks that you need to accomplish and keeps track of when they're due.

Responsive Design!

This means you can access your Todo list via any device or computer. iPhone, iPad, Droid Phones, Tablets, Computers, Laptops, you name it and you can access your list. So, whether you're in a meeting, sitting on the bus, working outside, it's always possible to add a todo or get the next one on the list.

Key features:

  • Quick Add - Enter the Todo, hit enter.. you're done!
  • Define Priorities so you only focus on the important stuff
  • Add Categories keep track of the ones you delegated, or to link related items
  • Easy Filter allows you to quickly pinpoint matching item(s), or filter by Category
  • Specify a Frequency to allow items to repeat (ie - weekly todo)
  • Groups allow you to keep Work & Home lists separate. Have as few or as many groups as you like!
