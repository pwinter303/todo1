about us

We are a small company based in a small New England town. We started To Do Giant because we couldn't find the right site to manage our To Do lists. It felt a little like the story of Goldilocks and the Three Bears. We found some online to do sites that were so complicated it felt like you needed and engineering degree to get them to work. We found other online to do sites that were so simple that they wouldn't allow you to complete even the easiest task. We created To Do Giant to be "just right". Hopefully you agree!
