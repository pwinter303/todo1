Introduction... Why Import Todos

If you have a list of Todos on your computer or are switching from another site, it's easy to upload them to TodoGiant.com. This saves you time and effort since you won't have to re-type what you already have!

Steps:

  1. Get your existing Todos into an excel spreadsheet. The order of the columns is very important. Click HERE to to download a sample file
    1. Category: Determines where Todos are uploaded. Should be 'Home' or 'Work' (assuming you haven't changed them)
    2. Todo Name: Name of the Todo. For example 'Buy Milk'
    3. Due Date: When the Todo is due. Use mm/dd/yyyy format. For example: 08/05/2014
    4. Category: Category or Tag. Used to group several Todos
    5. Frequency: Valid Values: Once, Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Yearly
    6. Priority: Valid Values: 1-Max, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9-Low
  2. Save the Todo spreadsheet as a CSV file
  3. Click the green "add files' button on this page and select the csv file you saved
  4. Press the blue 'upload' button and your Todos will be instantly uploaded to the TodoGiant site
  5. You can use the section at the bottom of the page to delete previous uploads
